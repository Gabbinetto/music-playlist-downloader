  Add ID3 tags

ID3 tags are metadata inside mp3 files that define stuff like the song author or album.
These are often used by media players to display song information.
Turning this on will set:

  • The song's title to the video title
  • The song's artist to the video author
  • The song's album to the playlist title
  • The song's album artist to the playlist author
  • The song's track number to the video's index inside the playlist
  • The song's cover to the video's thumbnail

It may cause the process to take longer.
